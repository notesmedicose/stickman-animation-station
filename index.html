Welcome to Animation Station!

Creating animation is simple and extremely fun! Draw on a frame, add another frame, and watch it come to life.

Onion Skinning (35% Ghosting)

You can see a faded version (default 35% opacity) of your previous drawing directly in the background. Use this to trace the motion step-by-step!
